City Facts

Population

As of 2022, the United States Census reports the population of Seattle, Washington, was approximately 769,256 people.1

Year of Incorporation

Seattle was officially incorporated as a town on January 14, 1865, and later as a city on December 2, 1869.2

Region of the State

Seattle is located in the western region of the state of Washington. It is situated on the Puget Sound and is known for its iconic waterfront and mountain views.

Classification

Seattle is classified as an urban city1. It's the largest city in Washington State and is a major cultural, economic, and technological hub in the Pacific Northwest.

Average Income Level

As of 2022, the United States Census reports the median household income in Seattle was $105,3911. This is 27.9% higher than the median household income of $82,4003 for the entire state of Washington.

Did You Know?

Seattle is home to the world's longest floating bridge.4 Crossing over Lake Washington, the bridge connects Seattle to Mercer Island.
