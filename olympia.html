City Facts

Population

As of 2022, the United States Census reports the population of Olympia, Washington was 55,669 people.1

Year of Incorporation

Olympia was officially incorporated as a city on January 28, 1859.2

Region of the State

Olympia is located in the western region of the state of Washington. As the capital city of the state and is situated at the southern tip of Puget Sound.

Classification

Olympia is classified as an urban city.1 It's the capital of Washington State and serves as a hub for government, culture, and commerce.

Average Income Level

As of 2022, the United States Census reports the median household income in Olympia was $67,9751, while the median household income for the entire state of Washington was $82,4003.

Did You Know?

The dome of the Washington State Capitol Building is the tallest free-standing masonry dome in North America and the fifth tallest in the world.4
